Blackberry Pie

Description

Very easy blackberry pie recipe

Ingredients

  • Berries - 4 cups
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Pie crust
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Line the prepared plate with the bottom crust.
  2. Fill the pie and cover with the top crust.
  3. Brush the pie with milk and sprinkle it with sugar.
  4. Bake the pie until the filling is bubbly and the topping is golden brown.
