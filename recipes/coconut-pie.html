Coconut Pie

Description

A coconut cream pie recipe that's tried and true.

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Half-and-half
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt+vanilla
  • Pie shell
  • Topping

Steps

  1. Toast the coconut and make the filling on the stove.
  2. Remove from the heat, then stir in most of the coconut and the vanilla.
  3. Pour the filling into the shell and chill until firm.
  4. Top with whipped topping and the remaining coconut flakes.
